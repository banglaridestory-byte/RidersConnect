<h2>📱 About RidersConnect</h2>

<p>
RidersConnect is an innovative mobile application designed to bring riders together in real time. 
Whether you're commuting daily or going on long rides, the app helps you discover and connect with 
nearby riders traveling along similar routes.
</p>

<p>
With RidersConnect, you can create or join riding groups, share live locations, and stay connected 
throughout your journey. The app transforms solo rides into shared experiences, enhancing both 
safety and enjoyment.
</p>

<p>
Built with a strong focus on community, RidersConnect empowers riders to communicate, coordinate, 
and explore together—making every ride smarter and more social.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>✨ Key Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>🔍 Discover nearby riders in real time</li>
